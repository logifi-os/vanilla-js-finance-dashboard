PRIVACY & LICENSE AGREEMENT

Your Privacy & Security

1. Zero-Knowledge Architecture: Logifi is a 100% offline, local-first operating system. Your data is stored exclusively in your device's browser memory (IndexedDB/LocalStorage). We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data.

2. Military-Grade Encryption: Your private Diary entries, Vault documents, and Payslips are secured using AES-GCM 256-bit encryption. The decryption key is derived locally from your PIN using the Web Crypto API. We never store your raw PIN or decryption keys on any server.

Important User Notices

1. Data Sovereignty: Because your data is stored locally, you are solely responsible for it. If you clear your browser cache or uninstall your browser without exporting a backup, your data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered by the developer.

2. Backup Responsibility: Please use the 'Backup Data' function in the Settings tab regularly to secure your financial and personal history in an external location of your choosing.

3. Optional Cloud Backup: If you choose to use the 'Sync to Google Drive' feature, your encrypted backup is transmitted directly from your device to your personal Google account. We do not intercept, monitor, or have any access to this transmission or your Google Drive data.

About Logifi OS

Logifi is a premium, zero-knowledge Personal Operating System (POS) engineered for complete digital autonomy. Built to save you time and protect your data, Logifi unifies your life into one secure interface:

  • Zero-Knowledge Vault: Military-grade AES-GCM 256-bit encryption secured by hardware Biometrics (Face ID/Fingerprint).
  • Financial Intelligence: Track income, crush loans with the Debt Snowball Engine, and forecast your True Runway.
  • High-Speed Tracking: Log daily habits instantly with Smart Autofill Templates and One-Tap Actions.
  • Vehicle Fleet: Calculate live Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) alongside MOT, Tax, and Insurance deadlines.
  • Encrypted Digital Keyring: Store passwords and private journal entries securely offline.

Logifi runs entirely locally on your device. Your data never touches external servers. Upgrade to PRO to unlock advanced financial engines, biometrics, and unrestricted vault storage.
